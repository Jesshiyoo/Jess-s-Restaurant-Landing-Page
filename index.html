Welcome to Jesshiyoo's Coffee House

Your go-to spot for a good cup of coffee. Sit back, relax, and enjoy every sip!

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

We pick good quality coffee beans from different parts of the world. Every cup is made with care by our baristas to make sure it tastes just right — rich, smooth, and full of flavor.

Our Favorites

  • Catpresso - Small but strong, just like a cat with attitude
  • Chillppuccino - Creamy, smooth, and perfect for lazy days
  • Overflow Catmeriano - Big, bold, and overflowing with good vibes

Our Story

Jesshiyoo's Coffee House started in 2026, built by a student who loves coffee and cats. It's a simple place where you can relax, have a great drink, and feel right at home.
